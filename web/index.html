Welcome to Aducj1910's T20 Simulator. Note that this is still in its pre-release stage so there may be bugs. There are currently 3 modes -

  • Custom Game -

    Here you can play a game between any two teams, they can be IPL teams or your own custom teams.

  • IPL -

    Simulate the entire IPL ball by ball

  • Remaining IPL -

    Simulate the remainder of the suspended IPL 2021
